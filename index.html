Welcome to TaurusTech

This is my workshop on the web. TaurusTech exists to document what I am building, breaking, and learning as I move deeper into software engineering, systems administration, and cybersecurity.

I grew up alongside the early internet. Dial up tones, CRT monitors, and software that demanded you understand it instead of just tapping buttons. That mindset stuck. I care about how systems work under the hood, not just how they look on the surface.

Here you will find project write ups, home lab experiments, WGU coursework notes, and tools I am actively developing. Nothing here is theoretical. If it is listed, it has been built, tested, or broken at least once.

This site is always evolving. Stable enough to use. Never finished.
